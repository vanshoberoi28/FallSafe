Fall Prevention Mechanism of Bicycle

The fall prevention mechanism of a bicycle model is a sophisticated system designed to maintain stability and balance, incorporating elements such as weight distribution, frame geometry, and braking systems to minimize the risk of accidents and spills.

INTRODUCTION

Problem Definition

The possibility of falling and losing control when riding is a major safety worry that prevents many people from utilising bicycles for transportation or enjoyment.The key issue is to create an integrated system that improves rider stability and safety by preventing falls and retaining control under a variety of riding scenarios.

Need Identification

It includes Safety Concerns, Limitations, Technological Advances, Market desire and Consumer Expectations, Regulatory and Industry Standards.
